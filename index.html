How to Play

Use the arrow keys to move Pac-Man through the maze. Eat all the pellets to complete the level.

Watch out for ghosts! If they catch you, you'll lose a life. Eat power pellets to turn the tables and chase the ghosts.

Controls:

  • Arrow keys or WASD to move
  • P to pause/resume
  • M to toggle sound
  • Space to start/restart
